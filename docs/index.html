PHI Protection

HIPAA-Aware
By Design

FELLITO was built from day one with healthcare compliance in mind. Patient data never enters the system — enforced at the AI level, not just the UI.

🚫
PHI Hard Block

If any message appears to contain patient names, MRNs, DOBs, insurance IDs, or clinical records, the AI immediately refuses and returns a compliance warning. No override exists.

📸
Screenshot Detection

PrintScreen keystrokes, clipboard copy events, and visibility-change patterns (tab switching for screen capture) are detected and logged with timestamp and session ID.

🔗
Ephemeral Access Links

Temp links expire after the Go-Live session. Each link is single-use, tied to a session JWT, and can be revoked by the admin at any time.

🛡️
Workflow-Only Scope

The AI is explicitly scoped to Epic workflow documentation, orientation materials, and build docs. It cannot and will not engage with clinical decision-making or patient data.

Compliance Posture
